About Me

I am passionate about working in a way to help improve the world and the lives of people within it. Wherever it's deployed, I think technology can be leveraged to support one another and make transformations for the betterment of society.

I grew up in Chapel Hill, North Carolina with a love for technology. I focused my studies towards computers early and ended up studying Computer Science at Appalachian State University. From here I would go on to Lafayette, Louisiana to build financial ERP software with CGI Group. While in Lafayette, I started to explore the possibilities of pairing software development with education and started a program to teach computer hardware and coding to local middle school students.

After a couple years in this position, I realized it was time for me to explore the bigger world and gain a better understanding of the different people within it. I spent eleven months backpacking through Asia. I got to trek gorgeous mountain tops, dive into meditation and spirituality, teach English in rural communities, drive through bustling Asian metropolises, and meet wonderful people from around the world. I have recently returned to the United States and am looking for my next adventure within the realms of software, hoping to find a path that utilizes my skills to aid in our mutual development.
